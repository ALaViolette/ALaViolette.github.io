Women must be present

Women make up 59% of the US labor force based on numbers published by the US Census Bureau. When looking at tech giants such as Google, Amazon, Twitter and Mircosoft women make up 30% of their workforce. The number of women in tech postions at these companies drops down to 15.6% based on numbers released in 2015.

At Michigan Women Code we want to encourage women to be apart of change. This site is designed to help women who are interested or apart of tech grow.
